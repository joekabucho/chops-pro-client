The T-bone steaks come from the lumbar vertebra with areas of the stomach. Making this steaks you need a T shaped bone meat.To prepare T bone steaks this meat must be dry and you need soy sauce, garlic, olive oil, butter, honey etc.take all ingredients in a bowl and mix and divide them in three.Before 30 minute marinate the meat.After that you just need to fry the meat for some period of time.After frying you can serve it with garlic, sauce.
